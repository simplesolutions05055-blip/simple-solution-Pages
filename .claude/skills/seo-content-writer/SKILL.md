# SEO Content Writer

Long-form, E-E-A-T-grade SEO content for Google Israel and international markets. Output is a publication-ready article: full body + metadata + JSON-LD schema + internal-link plan.

## When to use

Trigger phrases:
- "תכתוב לי מאמר", "מאמר SEO", "פוסט בלוג", "מאמר עוגן", "תוכן לאתר", "כתיבת תוכן", "pillar page", "מדריך מלא ל..."
- "write SEO article", "long-form content", "blog post for SEO", "ultimate guide", "comparison article"

**Don't use for:** ad copy (use `marketing-copywriting`), landing-page hero copy (use `ultra-premium-landing-page`), brand voice docs (use `brand-book-creator`).

## Philosophy — why most Hebrew SEO content fails in 2026

Google's Helpful Content System (rolled into core since 2024) and the March 2024 spam update destroyed two decades of keyword-stuffed sludge. The new moat is **E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust.

- **Depth beats length.** 1,500 useful words outranks 4,000 padded words.
- **Originality beats coverage.** One unique data point or first-hand client story is worth ten paragraphs of summarized Wikipedia.
- **Real authors beat "Admin".** Bylines, photos, credentials, LinkedIn — Google measures this.
- **Helpfulness beats keyword density.** Answer the searcher's actual question in the first 100 words, then earn the long read.
- **AI-assisted is fine.** Google has stated this explicitly. AI sludge is not. The differentiator is human editing + real expertise injected.

## Discovery brief — collect BEFORE writing

Ask in one message. If user provided some answers, only ask the gaps.

1. **Primary keyword** — exact phrase user wants to rank for
2. **Secondary keywords** — 3-5 LSI variations
3. **Search intent** — informational / commercial-investigation / transactional / local
4. **Target audience** — beginner / intermediate / expert; B2C / B2B
5. **Client's unique angle** — proprietary data, case study, expert opinion, hot take
6. **Target word count** — 600 / 1500 / 3000 / 5000+
7. **Language** — Hebrew (RTL) / English / both
8. **Top 3 competitors to outperform** — URLs of currently ranking pages
9. **Internal pages to link to** — 5-10 URLs on the client's site
10. **Schema type** — Article, BlogPosting, HowTo, FAQPage, LocalBusiness
11. **Author byline + credentials** — name, photo URL, title, LinkedIn (E-E-A-T critical)
12. **Publish date + last-updated date**

## Keyword strategy framework

| Placement | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| H1 | Primary keyword + benefit + year if relevant |
| First 100 words | Primary keyword once, natural |
| URL slug | Short, keyword, English transliteration when cleaner |
| Meta title | 50-60 chars, keyword near front + brand at end |
| Meta description | 150-160 chars, keyword + CTA hook |
| Image alt | Hebrew description with keyword variant |
| H2s | Secondary keyword variations, one per H2 |
| Body | Long-tails (8-15) sprinkled naturally |

**Hebrew search behavior to design for:**
- Shorter queries than English (avg 2.4 words vs 3.2)
- ניקוד inconsistency — write for both "שיווק דיגיטלי" AND "שיווק דיגיטאלי", "אינטרנט" AND "אנטרנט"
- Spelling variants — "מייל" / "אימייל" / "דוא״ל"
- English brand names in Latin — "Shopify", "WordPress" stay LTR inside Hebrew text
- Hebrew market is small: 200 monthly searches is often a great target — don't dismiss low-volume terms

**SERP feature targeting:**
- **Featured snippet**: 40-60 word direct answer in para 1 right after H1
- **People Also Ask**: include 5-8 PAA questions as FAQ section with concise answers
- **Image pack**: original visuals with Hebrew alt text + keyword in filename
- **Video pack**: embed a YouTube video if available
- **Site links**: clean H2 hierarchy with descriptive anchor text

## Search-intent matching

Different intents demand different formats. Match or lose.

| Intent | Trigger words (HE) | Format |
|--------|--------------------|--------|
| **Informational** | "מהו", "איך", "מדריך", "למה" | Long-form educational + definitions + how-to + diagrams |
| **Commercial investigation** | "הכי טוב", "לעומת", "ביקורות", "מומלץ" | Comparison table + ranked list + pros/cons + verdict |
| **Transactional** | "למכירה", "זול", "מחיר", "קנה" | Product-page-style + social proof + CTA + offer stack |

## Article architecture by length

### Short (600-1000 words) — news, quick answer
TL;DR (50 words) → 3-5 H2 sections → mini-FAQ (3 Qs) → CTA

### Medium (1500-2500 words) — standard blog post (default)
Featured-snippet paragraph → intro (100 words) → TOC → 6-10 H2 sections (with H3s, tables, lists) → FAQ (5-8 Qs) → conclusion + CTA

### Long (3000-5000 words) — pillar page
Executive summary → sticky TOC → 10-15 chapters → original data section → 1-2 case studies → tools/calculators/templates → expanded FAQ (10+ Qs) → related articles grid → author bio with credentials

### Mega (5000+ words) — ultimate guide
Chaptered with sticky TOC, sometimes broken into multi-page format (chapter-1/, chapter-2/) with prev/next nav, downloadable PDF version, schema linking all chapters.

## Section-by-section Hebrew SEO framework

### H1 — the only one
- Format: `{keyword} {benefit} {year/qualifier}`
- Example: `שיווק דיגיטלי לעסקים קטנים: המדריך המלא ל-2026`
- 50-70 chars. Power words allowed: "המדריך המלא", "כל מה שצריך לדעת", "מ-א׳ ועד ת׳"

### Intro (80-120 words)
- Sentence 1: name the reader's pain or question
- Sentence 2-3: featured-snippet target — 40-60 word direct answer to the search query
- Sentence 4-5: who this guide is for + what they'll get + signal of credibility (real client data, years of experience)

### TOC (medium+)
Anchor links to all H2s. Plain `<ul>` with `<a href="#section-slug">`.

### H2 sections
Every section follows: **definition/insight (1 sentence) → unpack with bullets/table/example → mini-CTA or transition.**

Break up every 200-300 words with:
- **Tables** — comparison, pros/cons, pricing, specs
- **Bulleted lists** — 3-7 items, parallel structure
- **Callout boxes** — "💡 טיפ מקצועי", "⚠️ אזהרה", "📊 נתון מהשטח"
- **Original visual** — diagram, screenshot, custom chart with `<figure><figcaption>`
- **Pull quotes** — from named experts

### FAQ section (mandatory for medium+)
- 5-8 People Also Ask questions
- Each answered in 40-60 words (Google snippet sweet spot)
- Marked up with FAQPage schema
- Phrase questions exactly as users search ("איך אני מתחיל..." not "ההתחלה של...")

### Conclusion + CTA
- Summarize the 3 most important takeaways
- Soft CTA: link to related pillar / consultation / lead magnet
- Don't write "לסיכום" / "In conclusion" — boring AI tell. Open the conclusion with a re-statement of the core insight or a forward-looking sentence.

| **Expertise** | Author byline with credentials (תארים, שנות ניסיון, ספרים שכתב). Quote named Israeli SEO/marketing voices: יואב משעלי, רן בר זיק, חנן בן שמואל, נטעלי שטיינברג. Link to peer-reviewed studies, .ac.il sources |

## Hebrew language style guide

- **Register**: plain modern Hebrew. Avoid לשון מקראית unless brand demands it.
- **Sentence length**: average 12-18 words. Mix short (5-8) and longer (20-25) for rhythm.
- **Voice**: active. Israeli ear hates עברית פסיבית ("נעשתה בדיקה" → "בדקנו").
- **Address**: second person — "אתה" / "את" / "אתם". Direct, never royal "אנו".
- **LTR pockets**: numbers, %, $, ₪, English brand names, URLs. Use proper Unicode bidi or `<span dir="ltr">`.
- **Punctuation**: Hebrew uses standard punctuation; mind RTL placement.
- **Geresh/Gershayim**: ׳ ״ for acronyms (ארה״ב, צה״ל) — not regular quote marks.
- **Numbers**: spell out one through ten; numerals for 11+. Years always numerals.

### Cliches to ban
- "בעידן הדיגיטלי"
- "בעולם של היום"
- "המומחים מסכימים" (vague — name them)
- "חשוב לציין ש..." (filler)
- "כפי שראינו..." (AI tell)
- "כדאי לדעת ש..." (filler)
- "לסיכום" (boring)
- "במאמר זה נסקור..." (preachy intro)

## Content velocity & topic cluster strategy

- **Topic cluster model**: 1 pillar page (3,000-5,000 words) + 5-15 supporting articles, all internally linking back to the pillar with descriptive anchor text. Pillar links out to each supporter.
- **Velocity**:
  - New site (0-6 months): 1-2 articles/week, focus on long-tail
  - Established (6+ months, DR 20+): 4-8 articles/week
- **Refresh cadence**: top 20% performing articles updated every 6-12 months. Update "dateModified", add a new section, refresh stats, re-promote. Often beats writing new content.
- **Internal-link discipline**: every new article links to 3-8 existing pages; every old article gets at least one new inbound link as the site grows.

## AI usage discipline

Claude or other LLMs can DRAFT. They cannot SUBSTITUTE for the human editor. Required human pass before publish:

1. **Inject real client data** — numbers from Gili's actual campaigns, specific examples with names (with permission), first-person experience
2. **Verify all facts** — Israeli stats especially. Wikipedia is not always reliable for IL data. Cross-reference with CBS (הלמ״ס), Bank of Israel (בנק ישראל), ministry sites
3. **Add unique angle** — opinion, prediction, contrarian take. "I disagree with the consensus on X because..."
4. **Tighten sentences** — LLMs over-write. Cut 20-30% of words on second pass.
5. **Strip filler** — every "חשוב לציין", "כפי שאמרנו", "ראוי להזכיר" must justify itself or die.
6. **Rewrite obvious AI cadence** — repetitive sentence openers, three-item lists everywhere, "delve into", "tapestry", "navigate the landscape".

## Anti-patterns — automatic rejection

- **Keyword stuffing** — same exact phrase 15× in 1,000 words. Penalized since 2003, still happens.
- **Thin content padded to length** — 2,000 words saying what 600 could.
- **"In conclusion, we have explored..."** — typical AI wrap-up. Banned.
- **Literal translation from English** — "Let's dive into" → "בואו נצלול" sounds foreign. Rewrite in native Hebrew.
- **Outdated stats** — every number cited needs a year. "67% של עסקים" without source/date = reject.
- **Faceless "Admin" or "צוות האתר"** — kills E-E-A-T. Real author or no publish.
- **Stock-photo hero** — generic shutterstock kills visual E-E-A-T. Use original or AI-generated branded visuals.
- **No FAQ on medium+ articles** — loses People Also Ask + FAQPage schema opportunity.
- **No internal links** — orphan page. Always link to 3-8 internal pages minimum.
- **Wrong intent match** — writing an educational guide when the SERP shows 10 product pages = ranking impossible.
